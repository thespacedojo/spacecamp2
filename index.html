Javscript Space Camp

November 10 - 13, 2016 in Corolla, NC

Can you imagine yourself with other like-minded Javascript hackers at a countryside retreat with awesome amenities for a weekend of fun? You'll laugh, hack, learn, cry (well, maybe not) and most likely play a crap-load of board games. This is your chance to form a deeper connection with others in the Javascript Community.

Day One (Nov 11th)

Unconference

The first full day at SpaceCamp we will be having a one-track user driven unconference. People can submit talks and we will all have a chance to vote on the talks we want to see. Want to submit a talk and show off some cool stuff you are building? Email josh@spacedojo.com.

Day Two (Nov 12th)

Open Hack Session

The second full day at SpaceCamp will be an open hacking session. We will have an area that you can submit the cool things you are working on (with your location) so others can come find you and help on your project. Don't have a project? Don't worry, there will be plenty of cool things to hack on.
